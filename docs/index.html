Instructions

All Mine is a puzzle game based on old 8-bit games like Boulder Dash, Repton, and The Pit. You must explore each level, digging through the earth to collect all the diamonds, before progressing to the next.

Beware of falling boulders and lizards hatching from monstrous yellow eggs.

Some diamonds are locked away in safes which need a key to unlock. And keep an eye out for the wandering spirits who must be guided into cages in return for a diamond …

Controls
  • Z - move left
  • X - move right
  • K - move up
  • M - move down
  • P - pause (and see a map)

The aim of the game is to collect all the diamonds on each level. Only then can you progress to the next one. Since cages and safes become diamonds when opened you'll need to take care of these too!

You control Bob with the keyboard. He can move left and right, and up and down. He isn't bound by gravity and can move freely around each level's maze.

Bob can dig dirt and collect diamonds by moving over them. He must avoid lizards and spirits — touching these is fatal.

Boulders will fall if unsupported, so be careful digging that dirt away. They also roll off any surface that isn't flat, including diamonds, curved walls, and other boulders.

They're heavy enough to kill, but light enough to be pushed.

Bob must collect all the diamonds to finish each level and move on to the next one.

Good old harmless dirt.

Eggs will crack if they fall from any height. Once cracked, it's only a matter of time before a lizard emerges.

Lizards are dangerous and will hunt Bob for breakfast. They're not invincible, however. In fact, they're just as squishy as Bob is.

Flat-topped safes contain diamonds, but they can't be moved until Bob collects a key. Then all the safes on the level instantly become diamonds.

Collecting the key opens all the safes.

Restless spirits are blind and never stop moving as they aimlessly wander the levels. Since they can't see, they like to keep the walls and other solid objects on their left.

Remember that their ghostly forms let them move through the dirt.

If you can guide a spirit into a cage it will reveal a diamond.
